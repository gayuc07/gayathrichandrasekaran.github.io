Government Spending

Government Expenditure refers to the money spent on investment, consumption and all transfer payments. This plays major component in the gross domestic product(GDP) of the country. Out of all, Health, security and education are the key priorities for any developed/developing countries. Analysis on military spending, health care spending and educational spending of the country show remarkable trends and pattern. This webpage projects historical trends and patterns of the G20 countries. G20 is the forum for international economic cooperation where leaders of largest economic countries discuss the financial and economic issues.[1]
