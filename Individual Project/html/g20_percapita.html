2017 - per capita Information



Military Spending

Highest per capita spending : $2130 USD


Top 3 Spenders
      1. Saudi Arabia
      2. United States
      3. Australia

2011 - 2017 - Saudi Arabia shows an increasing trend while United States per capita spending is decreasing year by year.


Indonesia marks the lowest military per capita among the G20 countries. However, there is a 22% growth rate in per capita spending from 2011 to 2017. Compared to other sectors, Indonesia's interest over military spending is increased.




Health Care Spending

Highest per capita spending : $10237 USD


Top 3 Spenders
      1. United States
      2. Germany
      3. Australia

Over the seven years, the United States, Germany and Australia have always shown an increased interest in providing health care services to the communities.


European countries have raised their bar in health care per capita expenditure. Growth rate is tremendously increased compared to other top spenders of this sector.


India and Indonesia have the lowest per capita health care spending. It's less than 0.01% compared to %Total GDP.




Educational Spending

Highest per capita spending : $2982 USD


Top 3 Spenders
      1. Australia
      2. United Kingdom
      3. Canada

*Above educational spending stats does not include china,united states,France,Saudi Arabia,Korea & Turkey spending



From 2011-2017, except India and Argentia, all the other countries showed a negative trend in per capita education spending.


Compared to health care, India show an increased interest in educational per capita spending followed by military per capita spending.
