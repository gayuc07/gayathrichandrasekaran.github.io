Comparisons With GDP


The Average military,health care and educational spending was about 10-15% of the country's GDP


The Spending figure highlights that G20 countries are more focused on the health care sector.


The United States tops both the health care and the military sector. However, military expenditure has declined substantially between 2011 - 2017. Health care spending grew by atleast 1% every year.


In the last 10 years, European countries show an increase in educational spendings compared to other countries.
