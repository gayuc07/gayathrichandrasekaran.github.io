Most of the countries have shown negative growth rate in educational spending and positive trend in healthcare sector


In the Military spending growth percentage, there were significant changes over the years in ranking among the countries. Two interesting facts from the data trend observed are, Except 2015, India has always shown an increasing growth in military spending. Saudi Arabia has shown a sudden fall of almost -26% in 2016. But they showed tremendous increase in 2017.


In Health Care spending growth percentage, Many countries showed a mixed growth percentage. However Saudi Arabia has slowed down their health care expenditure from 2011-2017. South Africa, Argentia, Russia are having significant growth in 2017.
